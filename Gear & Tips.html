Top Adventure Tips

Pack Light, Pack Smart

Use a 40L backpack for treks, prioritizing multi-purpose gear (e.g., a scarf-towel combo) to save space on trails like Annapurna.

Prepare for Altitude

Acclimatize gradually on treks like Everest Base Camp. Hydrate, rest, and carry Diamox to manage altitude sickness.

Plan Your Route

Use apps like Komoot or Gaia GPS for trail planning. Local guides in Nepal provide safety and cultural insights.

Choose the Right Footwear

Waterproof boots like Salomon X Ultra are ideal for trekking. Break them in to prevent blisters on long hikes.
