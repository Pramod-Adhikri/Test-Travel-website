Why Choose a Climbing Harness?

A climbing harness is the cornerstone of safety, connecting you to ropes and anchors during ascents. The Petzl Corax is versatile for rock, ice, and alpine climbing, ideal for Nepal’s rugged peaks or Switzerland’s crags.

  • Safety: Secure tie-in points for ropes and belay devices.
  • Comfort: Padded waist and leg loops for prolonged use.
  • Versatility: Adjustable for various body types and climbing styles.
  • Gear Loops: Store quickdraws, carabiners, and cams.

How to Choose a Harness

Consider these factors when selecting a harness:

  • Fit: Ensure a snug waist and leg fit, with room for layers in cold climates.
  • Type: Choose sport, trad, or alpine harnesses based on your climbing style.
  • Gear Loops: More loops for trad climbing; fewer for sport.
  • Weight: Lighter harnesses for alpine routes, heavier for gym use.

Maintenance Tips

Extend your harness’s lifespan:

  • Inspect for fraying or wear before each climb.
  • Clean with mild soap and water; air dry away from sunlight.
  • Store in a dry, cool place to prevent mildew.
