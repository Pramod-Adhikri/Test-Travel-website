Hiking Gear for 2025

Hiking in 2025 requires gear that’s lightweight, durable, and versatile. Our expert-tested recommendations are tailored for trails in Nepal, Switzerland, and beyond, ensuring comfort and performance.

1. Hiking Boots: Salomon X Ultra 4 GTX

Specifications

Material: Synthetic upper, Gore-Tex membrane

Weight: 380g (men’s medium)

Features: Contagrip sole, Quicklace system, waterproof

Price: ~$150

Why We Love It

Perfect for Annapurna’s wet trails, with excellent grip and waterproofing.

Pros

  • Lightweight and agile
  • Superior traction on mixed terrain
  • Easy lacing system

Cons

  • Less ankle support than high-top boots
  • Pricey for casual hikers

Specifications

Material: Nylon with DWR coating

Capacity: 40L

Weight: 2.4 lbs (1.1 kg)

Price: ~$180

Why We Love It

Ideal for multi-day treks in Nepal, with waterproofing and comfort.

Pros

  • Ample storage

3. Trekking Poles: Black Diamond Trail Ergo Cork

Specifications

Material: Aluminum, cork grip

Weight: 1.1 lbs (510g)

Length: 61-125 cm

Price: ~$120

Why We Love It

Reduces strain on Himalayan descents, with reliable adjustability.
